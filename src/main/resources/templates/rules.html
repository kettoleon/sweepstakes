1. Go to Manage my Bet to register and place your bet. You will be able to modify your bet until the day before the first match.

2. You will earn points each match according to the rules below:

  • +3 points for guessing the result (win or tie)
  • +1 point for guessing a team's number of goals (max +2)
  • +2 bonus points for guessing the exact result
Examples:
Bet Real Win or tie Number of goals Exact result Total
2-1 0-0 +0 +0 +0 0 Pts.
3-1 0-1 +0 +1 +0 1 Pts.
3-1 1-0 +3 +0 +0 3 Pts.
3-0 2-0 +3 +1 +0 4 Pts.
3-1 3-1 +3 +2 +2 7 Pts.

3. The bet is 10€ or 10£ or 10$ (Choose your currency)

4. There are awards for the three first places distributed as below:

  • 70% first place, 20% second place, 10% third place.
  • In case of a tie, the award will be divided in equal parts.

5. The deadline to do any change to your bet and pay the fees is the day before the first match. After that time, the page to set your bet will be disabled.

7. For transparency:

  • Before the start of the World Cup, all participants will receive an e-mail with everyone's bets.
  • This system's code is available in github if anyone wants to have a look.

8. During the World Cup, everyone will be able to check the classification.

9. Thanks for participating!
