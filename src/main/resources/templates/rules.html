1. Go to Manage my Bet to register with your workplace account and place your bet.

You will be able to modify your bet until the day before the first match.

After that time, the page to set your bet will be automatically disabled.

2. You will earn points each match according to the rules below:

  • +3 points for guessing the result (win or tie)
  • +1 point for guessing a team's number of goals (max +2)
  • +2 bonus points for guessing the exact result
Examples:
Bet Real Win or tie Number of goals Exact result Total
2-1 0-0 +0 +0 +0 0 Pts.
3-1 0-1 +0 +1 +0 1 Pts.
3-1 1-0 +3 +0 +0 3 Pts.
3-0 2-0 +3 +1 +0 4 Pts.
3-1 3-1 +3 +2 +2 7 Pts.

3. You don't need to pay anything, the company provides the awards.

4. There are awards for the three first places distributed as below:

  • 80€ voucher first place, 50€ voucher second and third place each.
  • In case of a tie, the award will be divided in equal parts.
