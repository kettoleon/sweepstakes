1. Go to Manage my Bet to register with your workplace account and place your bet.

2. You will earn points each match according to the rules below:

  • +3 points for guessing the result (win or tie)
  • +1 point for guessing a team's number of goals (max +2)
  • +2 bonus points for guessing the exact result
